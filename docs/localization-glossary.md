# Localization Glossary

## Voice Rules

- Keep movements in the Global South at the center of agency.
- Name systems of harm structurally: extraction, militarism, repression, co-opted elites.
- Do not use savior framing, managerial euphemism, or dehumanizing game shorthand for people.
- Rules text should stay clear and mechanically exact, but it should still carry pressure, dignity, and consequence.
- Arabic should match the force of the English without mirroring its sentence structure.

## English Style Notes

- Prefer `prepare`, `hold`, `force open`, `break`, `withstand`, `carry`, `circulate`.
- Favor short, active phrasing in buttons and alerts.
- Let system messages sound like field reports, not framework logs.

## Arabic Style Notes

- Target register: polished Egyptian literary Arabic.
- Keep buttons concise and direct.
- Use dignified political language: `الرفاق`, `الشهادة`, `التحالف`, `آلة الحرب`, `رموز الاستخراج`.
- Avoid literal calques from English such as rigid noun stacks or verb-first structures that sound translated rather than written.
